How faucet works?

Unifaucet stakes any erc20 or bep20 compatible token in a wallet, and offers 1% of
the reflections that wallet receives as available to Collect by anybody.

Connect your wallet.

Select a Token and
Network.

Click Collect and pay the gas to be awarded 1% of the token's reflected liquidity pool.

Investor Benefits

01

EASY ONBOARDING

Tired of having to explain how to buy your token every time one of your friends wants to try out crypto?

Unifaucet is a simple 1-click transaction where users are immediately launched into defi, getting your token at no cost to you.

02

ADVERTISE EFFORTLESS

The token list on the unifaucet main page consists of vetted, stable projects in the defispace.

Every faucet user sees the tokens available and knows that they are trustworthy projects.

03

CHEAPER, FAIRER AIRDROPS

Relaunch a token, or offer secondary rewards programs and avoid the gas fees.

Stake in uniswap once and your motivated customers can collect at theirconvenience, driving engagement!

04

PROTECTED INVESTMENT

Due to the mechanics of the unifaucet (seeUnifaucet Basics), your stake is secure andnever diminishes.

Recover it at any time; theonly penalty is reducing the amount ofreflection the faucet gets.

05

AUTO-FUNDING

The unifaucet funds customers by offering 1% of the redistribution it receives to anybodythat clicks "collect"on the main page. This protects your entire stake, which you can remove at any time.

06

DEFI ARCHITECTURE

Unifaucet offers staking and unstaking that works the same as Uniswap. Upon staking, RNBW liquidity tokens are granted that match 1:1 your staked token amount. You can unstake your tokens at any time.

07

UNIVERSALLY COMPATIBLE
